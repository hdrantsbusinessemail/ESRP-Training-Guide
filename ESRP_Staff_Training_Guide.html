ESRP Staff Training & Knowledge

Welcome

Welcome to the official ESRP staff training portal. This guide is designed to help you understand your responsibilities, learn staff tools, and become a fully certified ESRP team member.

1. Staff Overview

  • Maintain server safety and promote fair play
  • Respond to reports and incidents quickly
  • Assist members while modeling professionalism
  • Ensure rules are followed consistently
  • Support positive community growth

2. Staff Expectations

Professional Conduct

  • Respect all members and fellow staff
  • Do not leak confidential information
  • Maintain calm and maturity at all times
  • Act unbiased and fair in all situations

Activity & Engagement

  • Participate in trainings and meetings
  • Respond promptly to staff pings
  • Engage in staff discussions and operations

3. Staff Responsibilities

Moderation

  • Issue warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans when appropriate
  • Document all actions clearly
  • Maintain professionalism during all moderation actions

Support & Guidance

  • Assist members with questions and concerns
  • Guide new members in understanding rules and server etiquette
  • Step in during RP issues when necessary

Server Oversight

  • Monitor chats and RP scenarios
  • Process reports and escalate when needed
  • Communicate effectively with other staff members

4. Training Process

  • Step 1 — Application: Staff review of your application and qualifications.
  • Step 2 — Interview: Assessment of judgment, knowledge, and attitude.
  • Step 3 — Training Session: Learn commands, tools, moderation rules, and procedures.
  • Step 4 — Evaluation: Performance review on professionalism and decision-making.
  • Step 5 — Certification: Receive official staff role and permissions.

5. Punishment Guidelines

  • Verbal Warning: Minor rule violations
  • Written Warning: Moderate infractions
  • Kick: Repeated moderate issues
  • Temporary Ban: Major rule violations
  • Permanent Ban: Severe misconduct or safety risks

6. Staff Rules

  • Do not abuse staff powers or tools
  • Never punish without evidence
  • Maintain confidentiality at all times
  • Do not interfere in RP unnecessarily
  • Always act in the best interest of the community

7. Chain of Command

Staff Member → Moderator → Admin → Head Admin → Director/Owner

8. Completion

After successful completion of training and evaluation, you will be officially certified as ESRP staff, with the necessary roles, permissions, and responsibilities.
